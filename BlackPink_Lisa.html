Lalisa Manoban

Lalisa Manoban (born Pranpriya Manoban on March 27, 1997), better known by the mononym Lisa, is a Thai rapper and singer based in South Korea. She is well known as a member of the South Korean girl group Blackpink under YG Entertainment.
Pranpriya Manoban was born on March 27, 1997[3] in Buriram Province, Thailand,[4] and later changed her name to Lalisa.
